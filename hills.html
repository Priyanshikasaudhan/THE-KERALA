Hill Stations in Kerala:

The beautiful place of Kerala offers spectacular views and definitely charms its visitors. These include the beguiling hill stations loaded up with all- embracing tea and coffee estates, endless spice fields and an all-encompassing view on the fog clad hills. Regardless of whether on a family trip or a romantic experience, the hill stations in Kerala give ample chances to an energizing and refreshing vacation. So if you intend to visit Kerala, do enjoy a respite from the famous backwaters and beaches for a trip to one of these Kerala hill stations for the new mountain air and grand views.

  • Wayanad

  • Munnar

  • Idukki

  • Thekkady

  • Vagamon

    1. Wayanad – For Mist Clad Mountains

With its fog clad mountains and sloping rivers, Wayanad is a perfect destination for a weekend getaway in Kerala. The superb perfection, thick rainforest, astonishing waterfalls and spice estates give a great deal to see as well as lots of adventure sports to enjoy. Wayanad is definitely one of the most visited hill stations in Kerala.

The best ideal chance to visit Wayanad is from October to May.

    2. Munnar – For the aromatic Tea Gardens

The renowned hill station of Munnar is as acclaimed for its romantic plot for its rich tea estates. What’s more, if you plan well, you can see the valley turn blue during the bloom of Neelakurinji, which flowers just a single time in twelve years.

The best ideal chance to visit Munnar is from October to November and January to May.

    3. Idukki – For Natural Beauty

Idukki is one of the most nature-rich hill stations in Kerala and offers differing attractions like untamed wildlife shelters, spice estates, mountain treks, and elephant rides and many more. There are not very many hill stations close Kochi and Idukki happens to be the nearest one. Travel here, as this place is a perfect fit for a refreshing weekend.

The best ideal chance to visit Idukki is from October to February.

    4. Thekkady – For the Rich Flora And Fauna

Thekkady is on the wild side of Kerala. Well known for its Flora, this colourful hill station gives a lot of peace of mind. The 120 years old ‘Surki’ dam worked across Periyar, Poorna in Vedic speech, is a huge vacation spot. This reserve is spread over a territory of 777 sq. Km, encompassed by a lake over a zone of 25 sq km. The Country’s sole Tiger reserve, Thekkady, with its plentiful fortunes of tropical vegetation is a definitive treasury of many endangered species and rich inborn culture. Thekkady is a beautiful paradise on earth for the people who love nature in its wild signs.

The best ideal chance to visit Thekkady is from September to May.

    5. Vagamon – A Heavenly Hill Station

Surrounded by a range of thee hills, Vagamon is a place of intricate magnificence. With a mix of mountains, timberlands and hills, there is a great deal of personal brilliance to acknowledge in this paradisiacal hill station and a perfect weekend getaway from Kochi. Go for a nature trails with your cherished ones to see untamed wild oxen and elephants washing in the little lakes for a romantic evening. Vagamon offers a blend of both new and relaxing places of interest to visit on your vacation.

The best ideal chance to visit Vagamon is in summer from March to May.
